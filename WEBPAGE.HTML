SAALAR

Saalar

"Salaar: Part 1 â€“ Ceasefire" is a 2023 Indian Telugu-language action thriller directed by Prashanth Neel, starring Prabhas as Deva and Prithviraj Sukumaran as Vardharaja. The film, set in a fictional dystopian city-state called Khansaar, explores the complex friendship and eventual rivalry between the two characters against the backdrop of political intrigue and rebellion. The movie, which is part of a duology, is known for its high-octane action sequences, elaborate sets, and a storyline that delves into themes of power, brotherhood, and betrayal.
"Salaar" is heavily influenced by Neel's previous work, "K.G.F.", with a similar visual style and focus on a larger-than-life protagonist. The film's narrative spans multiple timelines and introduces a vast array of characters, including Shruti Haasan, Jagapathi Babu, and Sriya Reddy, among others. While the film is praised for its action choreography, cinematography, and background score, some critics have noted that the first half of the film spends considerable time establishing the characters and setting, leading to a somewhat slow burn.

THOLI PREMA

Tholi Prema

In the movie Tholi Prema, Varun Tej plays Aditya, a young man who is impulsive and unafraid to express his feelings, particularly when it comes to love. He meets Varsha on a train and is immediately drawn to her. Varsha, on the other hand, is more reserved and cautious, which creates an interesting dynamic between the two as they navigate their relationship. Their love story unfolds through different stages of their lives, showcasing the complexities and challenges of love and separation.
Varun Tej's portrayal of Aditya in Tholi Prema highlights his character's journey from a carefree college student to a more mature and responsible individual, showcasing different shades of his personality. The film explores how Aditya and Varsha's contrasting personalities and approaches to life lead to both passionate moments and difficult conflicts. The movie emphasizes that their love story is not a fairy tale but a reflection of real-life relationships with their ups and downs.

BAHUBALI

Bahubali

Baahubali: The Beginning is a 2015 Indian epic action film co-written and directed by S. S. Rajamouli, and produced by Shobu Yarlagadda and Prasad Devineni under Arka Media Works. Produced in the Telugu film industry,[16] the film was shot in both Telugu and Tamil languages.[17][18] It features Prabhas in a dual role alongside Rana Daggubati, Anushka, Tamannaah Bhatia, Ramya Krishnan, Sathyaraj, and Nassar. The first of a duology of films, it follows Sivudu, an adventurous young man who helps his love Avantika rescue Devasena, the former queen of Mahishmati who is now a prisoner under the tyrannical rule of king Bhallaladeva. The story concludes in Baahubali 2: The Conclusion (2017).
The film's story was written by Rajamouli's father V. Vijayendra Prasad, who told him a story about Sivagami, a woman who carries a baby in her hand while crossing a river, and a few years later about Kattappa, which intrigued Rajamouli. His fascination with Mahabharata and the tales of Amar Chitra Katha and Chandamama further fueled his interest in the story. It took the writers three months to complete the final draft. The soundtrack and background score were composed by M. M. Keeravani, while the cinematography, production design, and VFX were handled by K. K. Senthil Kumar, Sabu Cyril and V. Srinivas Mohan respectively.
