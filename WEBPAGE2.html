Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB), formerly known as Royal Challengers Bangalore, is a professional Twenty20 cricket team based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, that competes in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Founded in 2008, the team's home ground is M. Chinnaswamy Stadium. While RCB has a massive and loyal fan base, they are yet to win an IPL title despite reaching the finals three times.
